Rating System

Three ratings.
Infinite clarity.

A simple system that maps to how you actually think about footage.

O

OK — Best takes

The clips you'll definitely use. Show them to the director first.

K

Keep — Backup takes

Good enough to cut with. Not your hero, but not trash.

X

Reject — Skip

Misfires, blurry, bad audio. Marked and out of your way.
